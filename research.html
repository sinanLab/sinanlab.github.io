Research Interest

The field of mathematical modelling involves the creation of models to represent real-life phenomena that occur in both natural and engineering sciences. This process aims to establish and analyze the existence and uniqueness of solutions, as well as determine the stability of associated steady-state solutions. Additionally, the field involves characterizing various types of bifurcations, using chaos theory and fractional modeling chaos while applying analytical methods for partial differential equations and ordinary differential equations. This includes the use of integral transforms to further support the development of mathematical models.
