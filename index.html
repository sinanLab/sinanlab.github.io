Introduction

Welcome to my personal webpage! I am a mathematician with a strong academic background and a passion for applying mathematics to real-world problems. I earned my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics from the University of Swat and completed my Master's at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) on a fully funded scholarship. My research focuses on mathematical biology, particularly modeling infectious diseases using dynamical systems.
